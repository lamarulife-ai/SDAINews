## About the app

SD AI News aggregates publicly available headlines about artificial intelligence from a fixed list of established publishers and public RSS / JSON endpoints (MarkTechPost, Artificial Intelligence News, Unite.AI, TechCrunch, The Verge, VentureBeat, Wired, Ars Technica, Reddit, OKSURF, Google News RSS). It does **not** produce original journalism, does **not** edit headlines, and does **not** rank or comment on the news it displays. Each card links back to the original publisher.

The app collects no personal data and uses no third-party SDKs. See the [Privacy Policy](./SDAI_Privacy) for full detail.

---

## Editorial standards

Because SD AI News is purely an aggregator:

- We do not employ or commission journalists.
- We do not write or edit article content.
- We do not select or rank stories by hand; ordering is automatic, by recency.
- We have no advertising relationships with any of the publishers we link to.

If a publisher requests that their feed no longer be aggregated, please email lamarulife@gmail.com and we will remove the source in the next release.

---

## Corrections & take-down requests

We do not host article content. To request a correction or take-down of an article that appears in the feed, contact the publisher directly — we link back to their original article on every card.

If you believe the app itself is displaying something incorrectly (wrong title, missing source, broken image), email lamarulife@gmail.com with a screenshot.
