## About the app

Awarely is a free, non-commercial Android app in two parts:

- **News** — a clean, swipeable feed of World, national, regional, and video news drawn from public sources, ordered on your own device. No ads, no accounts, no tracking.
- **Scan to Know** — scan a product barcode to get a plain-language safety rating. The barcode is read on-device, product details come from **Open Food Facts**, and the rating is generated by **Google Gemini**.

See the [Privacy Policy](./SDAI_Privacy) and [Terms of Use](./SDAI_Terms).
