## 3. What the App is — and is not

Awarely has two parts: a **news aggregator** and **Scan to Know**, a product-safety scanner.

The **news feed** fetches publicly available headlines, summaries, images, links, and public video-news listings from third-party publishers and presents them in a swipeable feed (World / national / regional / video / positive news).

**Scan to Know** uses your camera to read a product barcode (decoded on-device), looks the product up in **Open Food Facts**, and sends the product details to **Google Gemini** (a cloud AI service) to generate a **safety rating and breakdown**. See §5A.

The App:
- **Does not** produce, commission, edit, fact-check, endorse, or verify journalism.
- **Does not** rewrite, summarise, or translate article content.
- **Does not** host article bodies or videos — every card links back to the publisher or to YouTube.
- **Does not** author product ratings itself — they are generated by a third-party AI model from third-party product data.
- **Orders your feed on your own device** from your reading signals + on-device content filters. This is not human editorial curation, and the underlying data is never uploaded (see the [Privacy Policy](./SDAI_Privacy)).

References to publishers, brands, or authors identify the source of an aggregated item and imply no affiliation or endorsement.
