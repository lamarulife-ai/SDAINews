# Awarely

**Be aware. Stay informed.** — a free, ad-free, privacy-first Android app that

## What Awarely does

### 📰 News
A swipeable feed of **World, national, and regional** headlines plus **video news**,
drawn from public sources (Google News, publisher RSS, YouTube channel feeds) and
ordered **on your own device**. Optional location tunes the national and
regional-language editions; share nothing and you get World news. On-device content
filtering keeps it suitable for a general audience.

### 🔎 Scan to Know
Point your camera at a **product barcode** and get a plain-language **safety rating**.
The barcode is decoded **on your device** (Google ML Kit — the image never leaves the
phone), product details come from **Open Food Facts**, and the rating and breakdown
are generated by **Google Gemini**. Past scans are kept in local history.

---

## Privacy at a glance

- The news feed is **device-only** — no analytics, no accounts, no advertising IDs.
- Your **location and reading history stay on your device**.
- **Scan to Know** sends only **product data** (never your identity) to Open Food Facts
  and Google Gemini to produce a rating.
